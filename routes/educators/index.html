1. Build your car

Form a team of 3 students. Build a car within the allowed. $500 budget maximum.

Don't break the bank and waste time fundraising for months. Get it done in days. Spend no more than $600 to be a full participant (parts and registration).

We don't allow you to burn cash to solve problems.

2. Host and join races

Build the track at school. All you need is some tape. We'll provide the design and dimensions.

A new track design is uploaded every 2-3 weeks. You have that time to run your car as fast as possible.

Host a race at your school or join forces with others for better competition. Build lasting connections within your community and with those far and wide.

3. Climb the rankings

Submit your team's best lap time with video proof. Keep track of your performance on the leaderboard.

Get rewarded for fast times, creativity, and team work.

APR doesn't require much to participate: a car, 3-student team, and some tape to build the track at school. We take care of the rest.
