Frequently Asked Questions

Is this only for students?

No, but we are focused on providing this opportunity for students. We understand interest is high, so if you’re interested, then you’re more than welcome.

Why teams of 3?

3 is just enough to spread the workload effectively to get things done and make meaningful impact. One person can’t do everything. 2 people is stressful.

Why is the maximum budget $500?

From our experience, it’s amazingly easy to burn through cash solving a problem. More money doesn’t always do it though. Creative thinking, ingenuity, and a solid team do. The max budget ensures that those three build you a fast car.

Why RC cars?

They’re the simplest and most affordable way to get right into self-driving car tech with no frills.

Is the Donkey Car the only car we’re allowed to build?

No. We encourage you to try other approaches. The Donkey is a well-known robust platform that is easy to start from. At the end of the day, you’re trying to build a solid, fast car.

Can we buy hardware from you?

No. We encourage you to source supplies as it will help reinforce your learning. If, however, you’d like assistance in doing so, then we’ll be happy to help.

Will you allow other size cars into the league?

We will have different tiers of races in the near future in which larger vehicles are involved. Stay tuned!

What are these maps?

They are track designs and dimensions in which you build at school. They’re designed to fit indoors. Only material needed is tape.
